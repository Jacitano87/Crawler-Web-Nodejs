				<div class="entry">
					<p>The BioBricks Foundation SB6.0 Conference is the world’s foremost professional meeting in the field of synthetic biology. It is made up of <a title="Sessions" href="/program/sessions/">plenary and workshop sessions</a> over three days. Each day will begin at 8 am, sessions will conclude by 6 pm followed by poster sessions on day one and two.</p>
				</div>
				<div id="latest-news">
					<div class="homepage-news-item"><div class="inner"><h2>Digital Conference</h2><p>SB6.0 was a great experience, and the <a title="BioBricks Foundation" href="http://biobricks.org">BioBricks Foundation</a> thanks all of you who made the journey to London last month. Thanks to you, this was our best conference yet.</p>
<p>If you were not at SB6.0, you can now see what you missed! The BioBricks Foundation is now making <a title="Digital Conference" href="http://sb6.biobricks.org/digital-conference/">all videos recorded and edited at the conference</a> available for FREE online.</p>
</div></div><div class="homepage-news-item alt"><div class="inner"><h2>SB6 State-of-the-Art Survey</h2><p>Please share your experience and perspective in this 15-minute <a title="SB6 State-of-the-Art Survey" href="http://biobricks.org/survey" target="_blank">survey to define the State-of-The-Art for synthetic biology</a>. Your participation will help promote ethical and open innovation in  synthetic biology research.</p>
