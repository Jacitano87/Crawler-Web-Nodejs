		<h1>Impact </h1>
		<h2> What We Do </h2>
<p><strong>The BioBuilder Educational Foundation</strong> puts current research into the hands of teachers and students. By converting exciting and ongoing research questions in synthetic biology into teachable modules, we increase interest, engagement, and understanding of science and engineering. BioBuilder lessons rely on design challenges. They are delivered through web-based animations, and in-class and laboratory activities. By making education more purposeful, we foster connected minds and build a savvy, creative community.</p>

<figure class="float-r"><img src="http://biobuilder.org/wp-content/uploads/2013/11/distributionChart.png" alt="annual report distribution chart" /><figcaption>Download our <a href="http://biobuilder.org/wp-content/uploads/2014/12/BioBuilder_2014_final_screen.pdf">Annual Report </a> for the 2013-2014 fiscal year</figcaption></figure>

<p>Current curricular materials are focused in the area of biological engineering and are well suited to advanced high school and early college settings. Future directions include the expansion of <a href="http://www.biobuilderjunior.org/">our middle school content</a> as well as the development of complementary materials for other STEM fields, such as physics, math and finance.</p>


<h2>Our BioBuilder Students</h2>
<p>We succeed when we’ve prompted students to ask authentic questions, to wrestle with uncertainty and to bring their best selves to address a real world problem.</p>
