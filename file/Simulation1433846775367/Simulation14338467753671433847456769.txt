                <h1><a href="http://haynes.lab.asu.edu/student-research-2/haynes-lab-students-present-research-at-ibe/">Haynes Lab Students Present Research at IBE</a> </h1>
                                <div class="recover"></div>
            </div>
			
			<div class="entry">
				<p><a href="http://haynes.lab.asu.edu/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/IBE2015_Cameron_Jimmy2.jpg"><img class="alignnone  wp-image-665" title="IBE2015_Cameron_Jimmy2" src="http://haynes.lab.asu.edu/wp-content/uploads/2015/03/IBE2015_Cameron_Jimmy2-1024x440.jpg" alt="" width="500" /></a></p>
<p>On March 5 – 7, <strong>Cameron Gardner</strong> (BME, SBHSE) and <strong>Jimmy Xu</strong> (BME, SBHSE) presented their work on synthetic biology at the Institute of Biological Engineering 2015 Annual Conference in St. Louis, MO. Cameron Gardner presented his human cell research with a poster titled &#8220;Isolation and Testing of Synthetic Polycomb Transcription Factors&#8221;. Jimmy Xu presented his work in developing a synthetic biology project kit for high schools with a poster titled &#8221;Massively Open Online Research for Secondary Education in Synthetic Biology.&#8221;</p>
<p>Cameron Gardner is supported by FURI.<br />Jimmy Xu is supported by NSF CBET.<br /><a href="http://www.ibe.org" target="_blank">The Institute of Biological Engineering (IBE)</a> is a professional organization which encourages inquiry and interest in biological engineering.</p>
