                        <h1 class="title entry-title">BioBricks Foundation Synthetic Biology 6.0 Conference announced: July 9-11, 2013</h1>                        
                        <p class="metadata"><span class="date updated">February 28, 2012</span> in <span class="categories"><a href="http://blog.openwetware.org/community/category/uncategorized/" title="View all posts in Uncategorized" rel="category tag">Uncategorized</a></span> by <span class="vcard author"><a href="http://blog.openwetware.org/community/author/jeffbrockstudio/" title="Articles by Jeff Brock" class="url fn">Jeff Brock</a></span> | <a class="comments-link" href="http://blog.openwetware.org/community/2012/02/28/biobricks-foundation-synthetic-biology-6-conference-announced/#respond">No comments</a></p>                    </div>
                    
                    <div class="content clearfix">
                                                <p>Dear SynBio Community Members:</p>
<p>The BioBricks Foundation is pleased to announce <a href="http://sb6.biobricks.org">The BioBricks Foundation Synthetic Biology 6.0 Conference</a> (SB6.0), which will take place on July 9-11, 2013 at Imperial College, London, UK.</p>
<p><a href="http://sb6.biobricks.org" target="_blank"><img style="width: 285px; height: 212px; margin: 0px; float: right; border-width: 0px; border-style: solid;" src="https://d2q0qd5iz04n9u.cloudfront.net/_ssl/proxy.php/http/gallery.mailchimp.com/b703a65bcfd5282409f58de2e/files/imperial_college.jpg" alt="Imperial College, London" width="285" height="212" align="right" /></a>This meeting will be the first time in two years that an open and self-defined global community will share, consider, debate, and plan efforts to understand life via building, to make biology easier to engineer, and work together so that the results of such efforts benefit all people and the planet.</p>
<p>SB5.0 was held at Stanford University in June 2011, with the participation of over 70 international speakers and 730 attendees from over 25 countries.</p>
<p>In July 2013, the world comes together once more to learn about and help define what is happening in the world of synthetic biology.</p>
<p>Please join us. For more information regarding the conference, please click on this link: <a href="http://sb6.biobricks.org/">http://sb6.biobricks.org</a></p>
<p>Registration for the conference will open later in 2012. We will let you know when registration officially opens.</p>
<p>Corporate sponsorship and exhibitor opportunities are now available. Please contact <a href="mailto:sponsorship@biobricks.org">sponsorship@biobricks.org</a> for more information.</p>
<p>We will send regular updates throughout the coming months, but please let us know if you have any questions along the way.</p>
<p>Hope to see you at Imperial College, London, UK in July 2013!</p>
<p>Sincerely,</p>
<p>Thi Thach<br />
Communications and Development Manager,<br />
The BioBricks Foundation<a href="mailto:thi@biobricks.org"><br />
thi@biobricks.org</a></p>
