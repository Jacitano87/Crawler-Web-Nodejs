																			<h2>Mail</h2>
<div id="attachment_1609" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignright"><img class="size-full wp-image-1609" src="http://biobricks.org/wp-content/blogs.dir/1/files/campaign-posters.jpg" alt="" width="300" height="200" />
<p class="wp-caption-text">Posters at the SB5.0 conference summarize the latest developments in synthetic biology.</p>
</div>
<p>To make a U.S. tax-deductible donation by mail, please make checks payable to “The BioBricks Foundation” and send to:</p>
<p>The BioBricks Foundation<br /> 955 Massachusetts Avenue, #330<br /> Cambridge, MA 02139 <br /> USA</p>
<h2>Bank Transfer</h2>
<div id="attachment_1608" style="width: 310px" class="wp-caption alignright"><img class="size-full wp-image-1608" src="http://biobricks.org/wp-content/blogs.dir/1/files/campaign-crowd.jpg" alt="A crowd at the SB5 conference" width="300" height="181" />
<p class="wp-caption-text">The SB5.0 conference brought together more than 700 researchers from around the world.</p>
</div>
<p>Contact us for information about wire transfers at holly(at)biobricks(dot)org</p>
<h2>Gifts in Honor or Memorial</h2>
<p>A contribution to the BioBricks Foundation in honor or in memory of someone brings special meaning to your gift. We will send notification of your honor or memorial gift to those you designate.  To make a gift in someone&#8217;s honor, please include the name and address of that person.  To make a gift in someone&#8217;s memory, please include the name and address of any individual(s), friends, family members or colleagues you would like us to notify.   <a href="https://npo.networkforgood.org/Donate/Donate.aspx?npoSubscriptionId=5282">Make an honor or memorial gift  online</a>.</p>
<h2>Gifts of Stock and Securities<strong> </strong></h2>
<p>The BioBricks Foundation welcomes gifts of stock and securities, which may also help a donor avoid capital gains tax on appreciated assets. All such gifts are U.S. tax deductible to the full extent provided by the law. In most cases, gifts of stock or securities can be completed through a simple transfer of assets between financial services firms.</p>
<p><strong>Stock gift from a brokerage firm:</strong> <br />Contact holly(at)biobricks(dot)org for instructions.</p>
<p><strong>Stock gift via bank wire:</strong> <br />Contact holly(at)biobricks(dot)org for instructions.</p>
