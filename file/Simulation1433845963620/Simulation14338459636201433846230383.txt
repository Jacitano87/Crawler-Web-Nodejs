							<a name="content"></a><p><a href="https://npo.networkforgood.org/Donate/Donate.aspx?npoSubscriptionId=5282"><img class="alignnone size-full wp-image-1666" style="margin-bottom: 0" src="http://biobricks.org/wp-content/blogs.dir/1/files/header-blog-campaign.jpg" alt="Donate Now: Help us raise $50K by Dec. 31, 2011" width="279" height="188" /></a></p>
<p><strong>Dear Friends of the BioBricks Foundation,</strong></p>
<p><img style="width: 334px;height: 281px;float: right" src="http://d2q0qd5iz04n9u.cloudfront.net/_ssl/proxy.php/http/gallery.mailchimp.com/b703a65bcfd5282409f58de2e/files/mailchimp_campaign_young_researchers_final.1.jpg" alt="The BioBricks Foundation helps nurture young talent in SynBio." width="334" height="281" align="right" />Here’s the latest news from the <a href="http://biobricks.org" target="_blank">BioBricks Foundation</a> and our fundraising campaign to raise $50K by December 31, 2011. As of today, <strong>we have already raised $35K toward our goal</strong>. So that leaves just $15K to go! Already made a gift? Thank you so much! <strong>Haven’t given yet? There’s still time to do so.</strong> <a href="https://npo.networkforgood.org/Donate/Donate.aspx?npoSubscriptionId=5282" target="_blank">Help us make it to the top!</a></p>
<p>The BioBricks Foundation (BBF) is a public-benefit organization dedicated to advancing synthetic biology (SynBio) to benefit all people and the planet. We work to create a sustainable future by supporting the development and free exchange of standardized biological parts that are safe, ethical, cost effective and publicly accessible. The funds raised in this campaign will support the BBF’s work for SynBio in technology, the law, education, and community building for the coming year.</p>
<p><a href="https://npo.networkforgood.org/Donate/Donate.aspx?npoSubscriptionId=5282" target="_blank"><img style="width: 208px;height: 54px;float: right;margin-left: 24px" src="http://d2q0qd5iz04n9u.cloudfront.net/_ssl/proxy.php/http/gallery.mailchimp.com/b703a65bcfd5282409f58de2e/files/button_donate_now.png" alt="Donate Now" width="208" height="54" align="right" /></a> If you work in the BioTech industry, attended the <a title="SB5.0 Conference Website" href="http://sb5.biobricks.org/" target="_blank">SB 5.0 Conference</a>, follow the <a title="BIOFAB" href="http://biobricks.org/programs/biofab/" target="_blank">BIOFAB</a>, or are one of 9,000 people who use <a href="http://openwetware.org/" target="_blank">OpenWetWare</a>, you are part of the BBF community. <strong>Please act now by <a href="https://npo.networkforgood.org/Donate/Donate.aspx?npoSubscriptionId=5282" target="_blank">making a personal financial gift to support the BBF</a>.</strong></p>
<p><strong><img style="width: 335px;height: 299px;float: left;border-width: 0px;border-style: solid" src="http://d2q0qd5iz04n9u.cloudfront.net/_ssl/proxy.php/http/gallery.mailchimp.com/b703a65bcfd5282409f58de2e/files/mailchimp_campaign_presentation_final.jpg" alt="At the SB5.0 conference, presenters covered the latest developments in SynBio." width="335" height="299" align="left" />Your tax-deductible gift of $10, $20, $50, $100, or any amount will have a big impact.</strong> With your help, the BBF will have resources to conduct such activities as launching OWW2.0, providing scholarships to young researchers for SB 6.0, hosting workshops on exciting themes, and launching initiatives to unite global BIOFABs and create a road map for the field of SynBio. The time to give is now. <strong><a href="https://npo.networkforgood.org/Donate/Donate.aspx?npoSubscriptionId=5282" target="_blank">Make a secure, online donation to the BBF today.</a></strong></p>
<p>SynBio has the power to transform our world. Let’s work together to ensure a strong, safe, open SynBio field. Please <a href="https://npo.networkforgood.org/Donate/Donate.aspx?npoSubscriptionId=5282" target="_blank">support the BBF mission</a>.</p>
<p><strong>Thank you so much for your support.</strong></p>
<p>Sincerely,</p>
