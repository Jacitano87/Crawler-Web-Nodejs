			<h1>Request an account</h1>
			<p>Thank you for your interest in participating in the consumer products inventory. Once submitted, you will receive an email from cpi@synbioproject.org confirming your request. Once approved, you will receive an email with your login and password information.</p>

<p>By crowdsourcing expertise our goal is to create a 'living' inventory for the exchange of accurate information on synbio enabled consumer products. Improved information sharing will allow citizens, manufacturers, scientists, policymakers, and others to better understand how synthetic biology is being used in the consumer marketplace. Registered users are encouraged to discuss and submit relevant data pertaining to synthetic biology products. Registered users can also update basic product information and add new products. All data will be reviewed prior to being uploaded to the public inventory.</p>

<p><strong>Please check your spam folders for an email from cpi@synbioproject.org confirming your request and account password.</strong></p>
